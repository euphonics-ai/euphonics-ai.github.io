Euphonics designs adaptive voice activation technology optimized for the next generation of hearables: We dedicate every algorithm to maximizing accuracy and efficiency for ultra-low-power devices.

Other solutions compromise between cloud and on-device processing; Euphonics makes no such trade-offs. For the world's most challenging acoustic environments, we deliver higher accuracy and longer battery life, enabling hearable manufacturers to create devices that are significantly more reliable and energy-efficient.

Our product

We focus on privacy-preserving, on-device learning for always-on voice activation. This means:

  • We support both initial deployment and continuous improvement.
  • We optimize for accuracy-per-millijoule first, and for latency second.
    • We'll offer the best accuracy-to-power ratio in the industry.
    • We'll provide competitive wake-word detection times of <100ms.
  • Our target applications, where we expect to achieve peak performance:
    • Hearable devices with ultra-low power budgets (smartwatches, earbuds, hearing aids).
    • Environments with complex, non-stationary noise (cafes, public transport, offices).
    • Use cases requiring continuous operation for extended periods of time.
  • We offer excellent scalability, supporting a wide range of microcontrollers and DSPs.
  • We give you granular control over the adaptation process; we know that expert users want that.

What our product enables

With our technology:

  • Hearable devices can maintain high accuracy in diverse acoustic environments without compromising battery life or user privacy.
  • Manufacturers can reduce false activations and extend device runtime.
  • Any hearable startup can afford to implement and continuously improve state-of-the-art voice activation.
  • The $100B hearables market (projected for 2030) can accelerate the adoption of voice interfaces across a wider range of devices and use cases.
